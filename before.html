competitions   Graphics Competition
Only one image per participant allowed. Maximum resolution 800x600. Accepted formats .png .tga .jpg .gif. Must submit atleast 4 steps of development. Must list tools and references used. Must not have been previously released.

Music Competition
Only one track per participant allowed. Maximum play length 4 minutes. Accepted formats .ogg .mp3 .exe .it .xm .mod. Must list tools, samples and references used. Must not have been previously released.

Combined Demo Competition
Maximum play length 8 minutes. All platforms accepted (bring hardware capable of running it or contact us in advance). Video and animation also accepted. Must list tools, samples, credits and references used. Must not have been previously released.

travelling   by car
Check google maps for directions coming to Guimarães. In Guimarães search for the Guimarães Shopping and Market Place. Our party place is close by. If you have trouble finding parking space you should use the shopping mall parking.

by train
The trainstation in Guimarães is at walking distance from the partyplace, please check google maps for directions.

by bus
The bus depot in Guimarães is at walking distance from the partyplace, please check google maps for directions.

by plane
Your best option is to fly to Porto, from there you can catch the Metro to the Campanhã trainstation, and from there you can catch regular trains to Guimarães. If this sounds too complicated for you, car pickups can also be arranged (you have to warn us in advance though). We have a few visitors coming by car from (or passing by) Porto, so just send us an email when you know at what time you will arrive at the airport and we will try to organize a car pool for you.
     
accomodation   Sleeping at the partyplace is available but the concrete floor isn't very comfortable. So if you're not feeling particularly hardcore we recommend renting a bed for 15 euros per night at Pousada da Juventude de Guimarães, it's in walking distance from the party place. Other hostels are available close by, please check the google maps of the area.
     
ludum dare   Indie game developers might notice that our dates coincide with Ludum Dare 23.
We are Ludum Dare friendly. In fact, a few of our attenders are planning on participating during their stay at Inércia Demoparty 2012!
